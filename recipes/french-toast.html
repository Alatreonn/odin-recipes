French Toast

Who thought a couple slices of bread would look (and taste!) so good! Now what if you grabbed some thick brioche slices and toasted them in some butter... My arteries scream! Ahh... they will never know... You wont know either, because we're gonna use normie sandwich slices this time. And normie ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 Egg
  • 1tsp Vanilla Extract
  • ½tsp Ground Cinnamon
  • ¼ Cup Milk (cups suck, as if all cups are made to contain the same amount of liquid)
  • 4 Slices of your normie Bread

Instructions

  1. Beat egg, vanilla and cinnamon in shallow dish with wire whisk. Stir in milk.
  2. Dip bread in egg mixture, turning to coat both sides evenly.
  3. Cook bread slices on lightly greased nonstick griddle or skillet on medium heat until browned on both sides. Serve with syrup if desired. Voila, easy French toast.
